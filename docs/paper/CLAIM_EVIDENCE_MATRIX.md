## Confirmation hierarchy

- Gate A throughput and total busy CPU are co-primary endpoints.
- Gate A throughput per busy CE is secondary and derived.
- Gate A composition contrasts form a Holm-adjusted secondary family.
- Gate B total busy CPU for `steal4-off` is the primary endpoint.
- Gate B softirq, per-core values, and diagnostic counters are descriptive.
- A confidence interval spanning benefit and cost is not an equivalence result.
